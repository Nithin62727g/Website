Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2026

At LearnFlow AI, we value your trust and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy details the types of personal data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and your rights concerning your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. Information collected includes:

  • Account Data: Full name, email address, password hashes, and learning preferences provided during registration and onboarding.
  • Usage Data: XP earned, course topics completed, roadmaps generated, and quiz scores.
  • Interaction Data: The chat logs you have with our AI mentor strictly strictly used to contextually answer follow-up queries.

2. How We Use Your Information

LearnFlow AI relies on your information to:

  • Deliver personalized AI roadmaps according to your goals and timeline.
  • Maintain and improve app functionality, tracking bugs and user flow improvements.
  • Update your streaks and XP dashboards securely.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. All backend requests are secured and your passwords and authentication tokens are highly encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt techniques.

4. Your Rights

You have the full right to:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • Object to the processing of your data for specific operations.

To exercise these rights, please contact support@learnflow.ai.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
