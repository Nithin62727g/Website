Help Center & FAQ

1. How do I generate a Roadmap?

Click on the "Explore Paths" or use the "AI Mentor" companion feature to type in any goal you want to achieve. Our AI will automatically generate a detailed module-by-module learning path customized to your timeline and experience level.

2. How does the XP and Leveling system work?

You earn XP by completing topics inside your generated roadmaps, and by successfully passing skill quizzes. As your XP grows, your profile level will increase to track your overall standing and progress across all your courses.

3. How do I maintain my streak?

Log in every day. The streak tracks consecutive days you have visited the platform (based on IST, Indian Standard Time). If you miss a calendar day, your streak will be reset to 1.

4. My roadmap disappeared, what do I do?

All previously generated roadmaps are stored locally as well as on your server account under "My Learning". If you can't view them, ensure you are logged in and have an active internet connection to sync.

5. Contact Support

If you have any further questions or encountered a bug, please contact us at support@learnflow.ai and our engineers will get back to you within 24 hours.
